ABOUT US


The internet is one of the most significant inventions, connecting a vast number of users worldwide. People use it for various purposes, with social media platforms providing easy access to information sharing. However, these platforms do not verify users or their posts, allowing the spread of fake news. Such misinformation can be used as propaganda against individuals, societies, organizations, or political parties. Since manually identifying fake news is challenging, there is a growing need for machine learning classifiers to detect it automatically. This systematic literature review explores the role of machine learning in identifying and combating fake news.
